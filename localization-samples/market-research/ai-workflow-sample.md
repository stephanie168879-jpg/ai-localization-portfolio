# AI Workflow & Productivity Sample

## Project Type

AI-assisted workflow improvement / document drafting / information organization / business productivity

## Scenario

This sample demonstrates how AI tools can be used to improve daily business operations, especially for multilingual communication, document preparation, and information organization.

The example is based on a fictional small company that receives customer inquiries in English, Japanese, and Chinese.

## Business Challenge

The company receives customer messages in multiple languages.  
The team needs to respond quickly, but the process is often slow because they have to:

- Understand the customer’s message
- Identify the key issue
- Draft a polite response
- Translate or localize the response
- Keep the tone consistent across languages
- Record the issue for future reference

## AI-Assisted Workflow

### Step 1: Classify the Customer Inquiry

AI can help categorize incoming messages into different types:

- Account issue
- Payment issue
- Language setting issue
- Product usage question
- Complaint
- Feature request
- General inquiry

### Step 2: Extract Key Information

AI can summarize the customer’s message and extract:

- Customer name
- Main issue
- Urgency level
- Product or feature mentioned
- Required action
- Suggested response direction

### Step 3: Draft a Response

AI can generate a first draft in the required language.

Example prompt:

> Please draft a polite customer support reply in Japanese.  
> The customer cannot find the language settings in the app.  
> Explain the steps clearly and use a friendly business tone.

### Step 4: Human Review

The AI draft should always be reviewed by a human before sending.  
The reviewer checks:

- Accuracy
- Tone
- Politeness
- Cultural appropriateness
- Product information
- Customer empathy

### Step 5: Save the Final Response as a Template

If the same issue appears frequently, the final reviewed response can be saved as a reusable template.

This helps the team respond faster and keep communication consistent.

## Example Output

### Customer Issue

The customer cannot find where to change the app language to Japanese.

### AI-Assisted Summary

The customer signed up recently and wants to use the app in Japanese.  
They need step-by-step guidance to change the language settings.

### Final Response Direction

Provide clear instructions, use polite language, and offer further support if the issue is not resolved.

## Benefits

This workflow can help the team:

- Save time on repetitive replies
- Improve response consistency
- Reduce translation workload
- Support multilingual customers more efficiently
- Build reusable customer support templates
- Improve customer satisfaction
